Galvanic Skin Response

Galvanic skin response shows a change in electrical resistance caused by the state of our emotions. Certain emotions such as fear and joy increases the activity of our eccrine sweat glands, the major sweat glands in the skin. GSR specifically shows the intensity of our emotions, rather than specific emotions.

Our Sensor

Our sensor constantly measures the value of the galvanic skin reponse of the user. It can easily be worn on the wrist as it collects data. Our website is able to take and store data from the sensor. With our user authentication system, only you will be able to access and modify your stored data on our website.
